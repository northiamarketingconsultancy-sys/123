Tree Health

Is Your Tree Trying to Tell You Something?

Trees usually warn you before they fail. If you notice any of these, it's worth a certified look — early advice is cheaper than an emergency.

🌲
Leaning suddenly?

A new lean can signal root or soil failure.

🥀
Dead or hanging branches?

Deadwood can drop without warning over roofs and walkways.

🪵
Cracks or peeling bark?

Structural or disease issues often show on the trunk first.

🍄
Roots lifting or fungus at the base?

Signs the tree's stability may be compromised.
